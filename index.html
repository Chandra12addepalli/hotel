Activities

Our hotel's offer many complimetary activities during your stay, depending on which branch you are in. The Sydney branch offer's yoga classes, spa hour, a free swimming pass, local tours, live music, movie theater, a gym, and an indoor karaoke. The local tours, spa hours, gym, movie theater and most of the other activities need a pass that can be purchased as extra at the lobby for ONLY $75.

Room's and Rate's

At our hotel, we offer various rooms with different prices (depending on your choice of room).There are: double bed rooms, single bed rooms, queen bed rooms, king bed rooms, our suite rooms, with an absolutely breath-taking view of the city, and the standard rooms. The double bed rooms, cost $250 per night (p/n), single bed rooms, $200 p/n, queen and king bed rooms $300 p/n, suite's at $400 p/n and the standard rooms, at $150 p/n (non inclusive of complimentary items, unless purchased at the lobby).
