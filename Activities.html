Activities





We offer daily biking tours around the city, which are complementary to your stay.

We also provide spa hours for our guests to relax and enjoy after a day out.

As included, we have a large pool, and due to the fact that our hotel has a beach view, and is connected to the beach, that flows out to a small fragment of the ocean that has been surrounded by underwater nets, for our guests safety.

We have yoga classes, with a qualified instructor, who is also a chiropractor, for a session of relief on the bones and muscles, to get ready for a new and exciting day out.

Extended onto the beach, a small portion is rented out by the hotel for sandy recreation, as well as boats, available for use with a qualified driver on board.
